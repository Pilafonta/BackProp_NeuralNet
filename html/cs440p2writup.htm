I was given 3 data sets crx, BUBIL, and lenses to train and then test my neural net.

 

Method and Implementation:

 

In my implementation of the back propagation algorithm, I had to write the train method in the NeuralNet.java file. We first evaluated our inputvs variable to populate our neural net with initial values. After I initialized my neural net I calculated the Beta of the output layer, by subtracting the computed output from the desired output. When training a neural net you start from the output layer and work backward, calculating all the connections and nodes to the input layer. This is why the algorithm is called back-propagation. After I calculated the beta of the output layer, I calculated the beta of the hidden layer, by getting the sum of w_j->k * output_k(1-output_k)*beta_k. After all the beta values of the hidden layers and the output layer was calculated, I computed the weight changes for all weights in the Neural Net. I used the equation delta(w_i->j) = r*o_i*o_j(1-o_j)beta_j. After that I added up all the weights for the sample inputs and change the weights.

The algorithm runs the training data a total of 300 times, and then runs the test data. The Neural Net then calculates the error rate of the of the training and the test data.
